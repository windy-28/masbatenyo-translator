Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information in the Masbateño Language Learning & Translation Tool.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Text you enter for translation is used only for that purpose and is not stored.

2. Use of Translation Text

All translations are processed temporarily and securely, either locally or via a secure connection to our language model provider.

3. Cookies and Storage

We may use your browser's local storage to remember your preferences or save favorite phrases. This data stays on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

If we use external translation models (e.g., Hugging Face), your text is sent securely and handled under their privacy terms.

5. Your Rights

You can clear your local storage or stop using the app at any time. No user accounts or personal identifiers are required.

6. Updates

This privacy policy may be updated. If major changes occur, we will notify users within the app interface.
